Insurance build-back: claim-to-completion

If you have storm, water, fire, or impact damage, we manage the construction side of the process end-to-end.

Step 1 — Stabilize & protect

Emergency tarping, board-up, temporary protection and coordination.

Step 2 — Inspect & document

Photos, measurements, and a clear scope for the repair work.

Step 3 — Plan & estimate

Materials, selections, schedule, and options.

Step 4 — Build-back & closeout

Coordinated trades, quality checks, cleanup, punch list, warranty details.

Important: We are contractors. We support documentation and the repair process, but we don’t provide legal advice or act as a public adjuster.

Common questions

Quick clarity for insurance-related repairs and rebuilds.

Do you handle insurance claims from start to finish?

We support the repair and rebuild side from documentation through completion. We are not public adjusters.

Will you meet or talk with my adjuster?

We coordinate around the construction scope, site access, and repair plans. Coverage decisions are handled by your insurer.

Can I upgrade finishes while doing insurance repairs?

Yes. We can quote upgrades separately so claim-related work stays clear.

How fast can you respond for emergencies?

We offer 24/7 emergency response for stabilization measures like tarping and board-up.
